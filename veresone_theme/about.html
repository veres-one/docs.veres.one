About Veres One

The Veres One Project envisions a world where people and organizations create, own, and control their online identities.

Governance

Board of Governors

The Board of Governors is responsible for managing the Veres One Project and overseeing the Veres One Blockchain. This includes ensuring the proper execution of the Veres One Project mission as well as collecting and distributing funds.

Maintainer

The Maintainer is responsible for managing the Veres One software. Functions include continuous security review, administering community contributions, correcting software defects, and implementing new features. The Maintainer is under contract with the Verse One Project.

Costs

The Veres One Network operates as a non-profit where costs are kept to an absolute minimum. As the network scales, fees for auiring and managing decentralized identifiers decrease. The absence of a speculative token ensures long-term price predictability.

Revenue

The Veres One Project receives revenue to fund the network in two forms: a flat fee for identifier creation and a percentage fee on what accelerators charge to write ditigal credentials to the network.

Board of Governors

The Board of Governors will consist of seven or more individuals who represent the cultures and markets served by the Board. The Board of Governors will be announced in Q2 of 2018.
